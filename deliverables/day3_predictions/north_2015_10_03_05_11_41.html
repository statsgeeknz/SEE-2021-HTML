Scheduled Utilities Forecast - SHEPD (North) - Saturday 03 Oct 2015

Day 1 of 5 (0600 Saturday 3rd October to 0600 Sunday 4th October)

 

General Forecast - Perth, Angus, Aberdeen, Moray Firth

A generally grey start with some drizzle possible. The overnight mist, fog and low cloud will the gradually lift to leave a mainly dry day with spells of sunshine. A dry night with clear periods, although there could be some areas of mist and fog forming once again.
General Forecast - Western Isles, NW & Cent Highlands, Skye, Argyll

Early mist and fog will generally slowly lift to leave a mostly cloudy day with a little drizzle possible. A lot of cloud around tonight too, with some patchy light rain or drizzle possible.
General Forecast - Shetland, Orkney, NE Caithness

Mainly dry today with sunny spells, but also a good deal of cloud around from time to time. Generally dry tonight with clear spells and variable amounts of cloud.
Synoptic Discussion and Confidence

An area of high pressure will continue to dominate the weather across the UK through the next 24 hours, maintaining the generally dry and settled conditions. High confidence.
Upland Forecast

No significant differences to the lowland forecast.

Day 2 of 5 (0600 Sunday 4th October to 0600 Monday 5th October)

 

General Forecast - Perth, Angus, Aberdeen, Moray Firth

It will be a mainly dry and rather cloudy day with the overnight mist, fog and low cloud slowly lifting. However, the fog and low cloud may linger all day across some parts of the region. Generally cloudy and increasingly windy overnight with some patchy rain possible.
General Forecast - Western Isles, NW & Cent Highlands, Skye, Argyll

The overnight mist, fog and low cloud will slowly lift to leave a mainly dry day with spells of sunshine breaking through, especially during the afternoon. Remaining generally dry overnight with some clear spells, but cloud is expected to thicken during the early hours. An increasinlgy windy night.
General Forecast - Shetland, Orkney, NE Caithness

A mainly dry and fine day with long spells of sunshine. Remaining generally dry overnight with clear spells, although cloud is expected to start to thicken during the early hours. An increasingly breezy night.
Synoptic Discussion and Confidence

High pressure will move away to the east allowing a low pressure system to move up from the south-west, causing the winds across the UK to strengthen. A front associated with this low will track northwards across a lot of the UK, generally during the evening and overnight. However, the front is not expected to reach northern Scotland until Monday morning. Generally high confidence.
Upland Forecast

No significant variations to the lowland forecast.

General Forecast - Perth, Angus, Aberdeen, Moray Firth

A windy and mostly cloudy day with spells of rain, which could become heavy for a time during the afternoon. Remaining windy overnight with further spells of rain, which could be heavy and prolonged for a time.
General Forecast - Western Isles, NW & Cent Highlands, Skye, Argyll

A windy and increasingly cloudy day with some outbreaks of rain, especially through the afternoon. Breezy overnight with further spells of rain, which could become heavy and prolonged for a time.

A mainly dry but increasingly cloudy and windy day. Windy overnight with spellls of rain.

Day 4 of 5 (0600 Tuesday 6th October to 0600 Wednesday 7th October)

General Forecast - Perth, Angus, Aberdeen, Moray Firth

It looks set to be unsettled through Tuesday with further showers or spells of rain, which could be heavy, frequent and prolonged with the risk of lightning. It is expected to be rather windy for a time on Tuesday.
General Forecast - Western Isles, NW & Cent Highlands, Skye, Argyll

It looks set to be unsettled through Tuesday with further spells of rain, which could be heavy and persistent with the risk of lightning. It is expected to be rather windy for a time on Tuesday.
General Forecast - Shetland, Orkney, NE Caithness

It looks set to be unsettled and windy through Tuesday with further spells of rain, which could be heavy and persistent with the small risk of lightning.

Day 5 of 5 (0600 Wednesday 7th October to 0600 Thursday 8th October)

General Forecast - Perth, Angus, Aberdeen, Moray Firth

Further spells of rain are possible for a time, but it is expected to turn mainly dry later.

Early rain is expected to clear to leave the risk of some showers through the day. It could become windy for a time too, especially in the far west.
General Forecast - Shetland, Orkney, NE Caithness

Outlook

There are indications that high pressure may try and build through the outlook period, bringing a lot of dry and settled weather across the UK. However, there is a lot of uncertainty concerning the timing and extent to which the high will build across the UK. There are also hints on some of the models that fronts may edge into the far west of the forecast region at times during the outlook period, but confidence is low.
